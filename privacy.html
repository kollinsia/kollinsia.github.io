Spozzle: Privacy Policies

Spozzle is designed purely for entertainment purposes and does not collect, store, or track any user data. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to ensuring your privacy while using the app.

Introduction

At Spozzle, your privacy is our priority. We aim to provide a fun and enjoyable gaming experience without requiring any personal information or tracking your activity. By using the app, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1
No Information Collection

Spozzle does not ask for or collect any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or device identifiers. The app functions independently on your device without any data transmission to external servers.

2.1
No Tracking or Third-Party Services

We do not integrate third-party analytics, ads, or tracking tools. Your gameplay and activity are entirely private and are not monitored or shared with any external entities.

2.2
Local Gameplay and Data

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. When you uninstall the app, all related data will be permanently deleted from your device. Spozzle ensures that your experience remains self-contained and private.

2.3
Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or to comply with legal requirements. We recommend reviewing this policy occasionally. Continued use of Spozzle after updates signifies your agreement to the changes.

3
Your Choices

For any questions or concerns, please contact us through the app’s support page. Thank you for choosing Spozzle—play with peace of mind!
